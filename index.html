WE CARE ABOUT OTHERS

THAT'S WHAT MAKES US HUMAN

The most vulnerable members of society are frequently the ones who are ignored. Governments and societies are not designed to provide protection for everyone. Unfortunately, this isn't the case for a lot of kids.


Children depend on their parents or other caregivers to provide for their safety, direction, and access to basic necessities.


Because of this, charities exist to fill in the gaps and offer specialized resources to assist the most needy. This is why we're available to speak with and help every kid and adolescent, as needed.

GIVING TO THOSE IN NEED

GENEROSITY IS OUR KEY VALUE

Your donation matters, regardless of how much you are able to provide, whether it is a one-time gift, a recurring donation, or the result of fundraising efforts.


So what if you don't have much extra cash? Old clothes that you don't wear, equipment that you no longer need and even food donations are welcome too.


Each donation helps our charity get to work and make a difference in the our community, because they pile up over time. We are always appreciative of any contribution, no matter the size.

Our Causes

HUNGER AND POVERTY

Fighting and defeating malnutrition and hunger remains our top priority. Helping the youth of today to gain access to a healthy daily meal is integral to securing a strong generation of leaders for our tommorow. Through this we give them the ability to have a better quality of life and be able to pursue opportunities that they wouldn't have been able to.

EDUCATION AND TRAINING

We aim to uplift vulnerable children and the youth in general by aiding them with access to education, vocational training and workshops. Education is a very important human rights, regardless of age, gender or location in the world, every person should have the chance to obtain knowledge, enabling them to use it to build a better tommorow for our world.

About us

Hope For All Charity Organization is a non-profit organization based in Windhoek, Namibia that targets vulnerable and and orphaned children by providing food and nutrition through soup kitchens.

It was started in 2006 as a passion project by Christian individuals that believe in helping their fellow neighbours, especially those in dire need.
