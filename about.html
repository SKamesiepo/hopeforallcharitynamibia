About us

Charity For All is a non-profit organization based in Windhoek, Namibia that targets vulnerable and and orphaned children by providing food and nutrition through soup kitchens.

The "Charity For All" organization was started in 2006 as a passion project by Christian individuals that believe in helping their fellow neighbours, especially those in dire need.
